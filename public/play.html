Tips

There are two ways to interact with the cube

    1. Keyboard - Each action corresponds to a different key. This method takes a bit of time to get used to, but you will develop the muscle memory with practice. The key mappings are listed below.

    2. Dragging - This method is slower, but easier to understand at first. Simply drag in the direction you want a face to turn. You can also do cube rotations by dragging just outside the cube.
